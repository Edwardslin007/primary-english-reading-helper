2. Ha ha ha! What an ugly old go-kart!

4. Great, Misty!
5. She’s first! Stop her!
6. Help! Oh no!
7. Ha ha ha! Now I’m first!
8. That isn’t fair!
9. Just a minute.
10. Hold on, Misty!
11. Woah! No!
12. Congratulations, Misty! You’re first!
13. Thank you!
